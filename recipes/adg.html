Aji de Gallina

Ingredients

  • Aji (Yellow Chilli)
  • Bread
  • Chicken Breast
  • Garlic (Ground)
  • Onion (Chopped)
  • Potatoes

Preparation

  1. Place bread on a blender with milk and butter then blend
  2. On a large, hollow pan, boil Chicken and the potatoes
  3. On a small, shallow pan with oil, fry ground garlic along with chopped onion and some Aji
  4. As soon as fully cooked (marble white), shred the chicken and add to the shallow pan with its contents
  5. Slowly add the stew from the boiled chicken to the shallow pan
  6. Add the blended bread to the mixture
  7. Add the potatoes to the mixture
