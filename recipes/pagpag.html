Pagpag (Filipino Style Fried Landfill Chicken)

Ingredients

  • Chicken from a landfilled field
  • Green onion
  • Tomato sauce

Preparation

  1. Boil the already cooked chicken to kill all bacteria and other parasitic microorganisms
  2. Refry the chicken
  3. Prepare the tomato sauce
  4. Add the chicken to the sauce
  5. Add chopped green onion
